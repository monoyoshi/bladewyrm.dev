A Discord bot that does a lot, from unserious things like laughing at people, to serious things like m*th. hotaru(n) is also evidently the predecessor to some of the other projects I have here. In a sense, he's like my sandbox...if sandboxes could be sassy.

Made using Javascript (Node.js) on a boring after-school November afternoon in 2016. He uses Discord.js to interact with Discord's API. And no, you can't add him onto your server yet (he's only hosted locally).
